Current Opportunities

Regular Volunteer

The heartbeat of our mission. Help with daily operations including food sorting, inventory management, and assisting with local deliveries to ensure surplus reaches those in need promptly.

Volunteer Coordinator

Lead the charge. Help manage schedules, guide new recruits, and ensure our volunteer teams operate smoothly and safely across all depots and distribution events.

Project Officer

Drive our long-term vision. Work on strategic initiatives, community outreach programs, and corporate partnership development to expand FoodShare's impact across the islands.

Looking for something more specialized? Contact us directly to discuss unique roles.
