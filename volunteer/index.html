Become a Foodshare Volunteer

Your time and effort are the driving force behind our mission to eliminate food waste in Seychelles.

What is a Foodshare Volunteer?

A Foodshare volunteer is a dedicated individual who assists in the critical logistics of food rescue. Volunteers primarily help with **collection and distribution**â€”picking up surplus food from donors (hotels, markets, bakeries) and delivering it directly to vetted community partners and those in need.

Roles can also include **administrative support**, **tech support**, and **community outreach**. Even a few hours a week can make a massive difference in preventing food from going to waste.

Connecting surplus food with those who need it across the Seychelles islands to eliminate food waste and nourish our community.
