Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 13, 2025

FoodShare Seychelles is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal data when you interact with our services, including submitting volunteer applications or general inquiries.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you use our services:

  • Volunteer Data: Name, contact details (email, phone, address), availability, skills, and emergency contact information collected via the Volunteer Form.
  • Inquiry Data: Name, email address, and the content of your message submitted through the Contact or General Inquiry forms.
  • Future Beneficiary Data: (Upon activation of the "Available Food" service) Sensitive information related to your needs and location, subject to strict anonymity protocols.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage your volunteer application, including vetting and scheduling.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To manage and coordinate the logistical flow of food distribution (once operational).
  • To comply with legal and safety requirements relevant to food handling and volunteer management in the Seychelles.

3. Data Security and Storage

Your data is stored securely using cloud-based services (Google Firebase/Firestore). We employ technical and organizational security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your personal data. Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to authorized Staff Login accounts.

4. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at the canonical administrative email address:

Email: admin@foodshare-seychelles.online
