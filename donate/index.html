List Your Surplus Food for Donation

Prevent edible food from becoming waste. We make it easy to list your surplus inventory for rapid collection by vetted community organizations.

What We Accept

  • Safe, packaged **perishables** (dairy, meat, cooked meals)
  • Fresh produce, fruit, and eggs
  • **Non-perishables** with a future expiry date
  • Frozen items that have maintained temperature

Donation Checklist

  • Food must be ready for pickup.
  • Clearly label all items with contents and preparation date.
  • Ensure all food is kept at appropriate temperatures until collection.
  • Provide an accurate weight/quantity to help us match recipients.
