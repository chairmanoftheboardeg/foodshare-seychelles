Account Access

Why sign up to claim food?

Creating an account gives you direct access to our Digital Pantry. To ensure our rescued food reaches real community members and to prevent spam claims, we require everyone to register. Signing up allows you to securely claim surplus food and gives our volunteer drivers the information they need to coordinate your delivery or pickup.

Staff & Volunteers

If you are an official staff member or volunteer, your credentials are already registered in the system. Please sign in directly on the right to access your management dashboard.

Note: If you cannot recover your credentials, please do not create a new account. Connect with an admin directly to reset your access.
