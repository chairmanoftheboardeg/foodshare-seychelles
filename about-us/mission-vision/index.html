Our Mission and Vision

Defining the purpose and future of FoodShare Seychelles.

Our Mission Statement

"To eradicate food waste and combat food insecurity in the Seychelles by operating a reliable, efficient, and technologically-driven platform that connects food surplus to those who need it, sustainably supporting community welfare and environmental health."

  • **Zero Waste:** Maximizing the utilization of all edible food.
  • **Community First:** Ensuring dignity and equity in food distribution.
  • **Tech-Driven:** Utilizing our platform for transparency and efficiency.

Our 5-Year Vision

"To be the single, indispensable network for food rescue across all major Seychelles islands (Mah√©, Praslin, La Digue), providing **3 million meals** to vulnerable populations and diverting **1,000 metric tons of CO2e** from landfills by 2030."

Strategic Pillars:

  • **Logistics Excellence:** Establishing dedicated volunteer hubs on all three main islands.
  • **Educational Outreach:** Partnering with schools to promote food literacy and composting.
  • **Policy Advocacy:** Working with the government to enact favorable food donation policies.

A non-profit initiative dedicated to sustainability and community welfare.
