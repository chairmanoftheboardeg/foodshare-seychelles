About FoodShare Seychelles

FoodShare Seychelles is a non-profit initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and supporting community welfare. We believe that surplus food is a resource, not waste. By bridging the gap between donors and those in need, we ensure a more sustainable and food-secure future for our islands.

OUR MISSION

To implement an efficient, safe, and transparent food rescue system that prevents nutritious surplus food from entering landfills, redirecting it to the plates of those who need it most.

OUR VISION

A Seychelles where every community is empowered by shared resources, and where the synergy of technology and compassion eliminates food insecurity across the islands.
