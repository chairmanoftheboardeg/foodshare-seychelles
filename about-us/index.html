About FoodShare Seychelles

Connecting surplus to need for a sustainable Seychelles.

Our Role in the Community

FoodShare Seychelles is a non-profit technology platform dedicated to solving the dual problems of **food waste** and **food insecurity** across the islands. We act as the digital bridge between businesses, farms, and individuals with surplus, perfectly good food, and the charities, organizations, and vulnerable community members who need it most.

Since our inception, we have rapidly scaled our logistics network to ensure efficiency and safety, making sure every kilogram of rescued food directly impacts a Seychellois family or organization in need.
