About FoodShare Seychelles

FoodShare Seychelles reduces food waste and supports food access by connecting donors and recipients while coordinating real-world logistics through staff and volunteers.

Our Mission

Rescue safe surplus food and redirect it to people who need it—efficiently, responsibly, and with dignity.

Our Values

  • Safety-first food handling and clear guidance
  • Privacy and data minimization by design
  • Operational accountability with transparent impact

How We Operate

Submissions are reviewed by staff/admin, tasks are assigned securely, and outcomes are recorded for impact reporting.
