About Our Mission

Transparency, Integrity, and Action in Seychelles.

Founded on the belief that no good food should go to waste while community members go hungry, FoodShare Seychelles operates as a bridge between surplus resources and local needs. We leverage technology to make food redistribution fast, safe, and dignified.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste across the Seychelles islands by connecting hospitality and retail surplus to local communities through efficient logistics and real-time data.

Our Vision

A Seychelles where sustainable consumption is the norm, food security is strengthened, and community welfare is powered by shared resources.
