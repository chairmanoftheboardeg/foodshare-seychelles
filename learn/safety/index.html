Food Safety Protocol

FoodShare Seychelles follows strict handling procedures to protect recipients and maintain donor trust. This protocol outlines acceptance criteria, storage, transport, and distribution safety practices.

Safety first: if an item’s safety is uncertain, we do not distribute it.

Accepted Donations

  • Food that is safe, unspoiled, and properly stored.
  • Packaged goods with intact seals and readable labels.
  • Produce that is clean and free from significant mold/rot.
  • Chilled/frozen items only if cold-chain can be maintained.
Note: “Best before” items may be accepted if quality is still good, subject to screening.

Not Accepted

  • Food with signs of spoilage (off smell, slime, mold on most foods).
  • Open/partially used products (unless a controlled program is in place).
  • Items without labels where allergens/contents cannot be confirmed.
  • Food exposed to unsafe temperatures for extended periods.
We may decline donations if packaging is damaged or contamination risk is present.

Temperature & Cold Chain

  • Keep chilled foods cold until handoff (use coolers/ice packs).
  • Frozen foods must remain frozen; avoid thaw-refreeze cycles.
  • Minimize time between pickup and distribution.
  • Separate raw items from ready-to-eat foods.
If cold chain is broken, items may be rejected for safety.

Packaging & Labeling

  • Use clean, food-safe containers.
  • Label items with contents, date, and storage instructions if possible.
  • For prepared foods: include prep date and “consume by” guidance.
  • Keep allergens identifiable (nuts, dairy, gluten, seafood, etc.).

Transport & Handling

  • Clean transport surfaces; avoid chemical contamination.
  • Keep food off the floor; protect from sun/rain exposure.
  • Use “first expiring, first out” prioritization.
  • Record pickup and drop-off times when possible.

Screening & Traceability

  • Staff/volunteers visually inspect items at pickup/intake.
  • High-risk categories receive extra scrutiny.
  • Trace donor contact where needed for follow-ups.
  • Log and safely dispose of rejected items.

Donor Guidance

If you are unsure whether your items qualify, submit a donation request with details and photos/description. Our team will advise and schedule safe pickup.
