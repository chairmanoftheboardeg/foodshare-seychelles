FAQ

Common questions about donations, claims, and volunteering.

Donate Now Find Food
Do I need an account to use the forms?
Yes. Login is required to submit forms so we can reply via your dashboard notifications. If you cannot login, email admin@foodshare-seychelles.online.
How do I claim food?
Go to Available Food → select a listing → choose Transport or Self Pickup → submit. You will receive updates in notifications.
How do I donate food?
Use Donate Now and provide item details, quantity, expiry date, and pickup location/timeframe.
How do volunteers get approved?
Apply on Volunteer Now. Admin reviews your application. You will receive updates in notifications.
Is the data private?
We use role-based security. Community members can only see their own submissions and notifications.
