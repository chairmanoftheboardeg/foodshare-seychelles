Our Story & Mission

Learn how Foodshare Seychelles is working to connect every island, reduce food waste, and nourish our people.

Our Guiding Principles

Mission

To eliminate food insecurity and unnecessary food waste across the Republic of Seychelles by creating a seamless, reliable, and safe distribution network between food donors and community organizations.

Vision

A Seychelles where every citizen has access to sufficient, nutritious food, and no edible food goes to waste due to logistical barriers.

The roblem We Solve

Despite our stunning natural abundance, food waste remains a significant challenge, particularly in the tourism and import sectors. Simultaneously, many families and vulnerable groups on Mahé, Praslin, and La Digue face daily struggles to secure consistent, high-quality meals.

Foodshare Seychelles acts as the critical link—a technology-driven logistics hub that takes surplus food from hotels, markets, and businesses and directs it quickly and safely to shelters, schools, and community centers.

How We Operate

Our operations rely on a dedicated blend of technology and human effort. When a donation is listed on our platform, our staff processes the food safety criteria, coordinates time-sensitive transport requests, and dispatches our volunteer drivers. We prioritize island-to-island logistics to ensure that surplus food reaches even the most remote communities without delay.

We are a registered non-profit organization dedicated to serving the entirety of the Seychellois population.

Our Mission

Connecting surplus food with those who need it across the Seychelles islands to eliminate food waste and nourish our community.
