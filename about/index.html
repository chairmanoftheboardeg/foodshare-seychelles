About FoodShare Seychelles

Leading the charge against food waste and hunger across the islands.

Our Story & Purpose

Founded in 2024, FoodShare Seychelles emerged from the urgent need to bridge the gap between perfectly good, surplus food and the communities experiencing food insecurity. We operate a streamlined logistics network dedicated to rescuing food from restaurants, farms, and markets before it goes to waste.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste to zero while ensuring every Seychellois has access to nutritious meals, promoting a sustainable and equitable food system.

Our Vision

A Seychelles where surplus food is efficiently redistributed, empowering communities and fostering environmental responsibility.

Meet the Team

We are a dedicated group of staff and volunteers passionate about making a difference. Our core team manages the logistics, technology, and community outreach.
